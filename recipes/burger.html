Burger

Wow! It's a burger which can be eaten!

Ingredients

  • Half of a bun
  • The other half of a bun
  • A circular slice of beef
  • A tasty sauce from Asda
  • Some lettuce I guess whatever

Instructions

  1. Take the half of a bun and the other half of a bun and put the beef circle inside.
  2. Do the same for the sauce which is from Asda.
  3. Put the lettuce on if you really want.
